From phone video to living memory

Three steps. Capture, Generate, and Revisit.

Capture with MemoLens

AR-guided shooting ensures spatial accuracy. Each loop forms the foundation for future 3D reconstruction.

Generate¹ via 3DGS Engine

Convert videos into realistic 3D scenes using open-source 3DGS reconstruction tools platforms.

Revisit with MemoSpace

Experience the reconstructed scene in VR. Walk back into memories through immersive exploration.

¹ The generation process is not operated by MemoSpace. Users upload MemoLens-guided videos to open-source 3DGS platforms for reconstruction. MemoSpace and MemoLens are responsible only for the capture and revisit parts of the workflow.

  1. 1 Use MemoLens for AR-guided 360° recording to ensure spatial precision.
  2. 2 Export your video and process it with an open-source 3DGS reconstruction engine.
  3. 3 Import the 3D scene into MemoSpace to relive and explore in VR.

From capture to revisit

Each stage works independently — MemoLens captures, open-source 3DGS rebuilds, and MemoSpace replays. Together they form a seamless bridge between memory and immersion.
