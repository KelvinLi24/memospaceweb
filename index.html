From phone video to living memory

Four steps. Minutes to set up. Awe on repeat.

Capture with MemoLens

Follow AR guidance to film a smooth 360° loop. Your clip doubles as a social-ready short.

Rebuild with 3DGS

Our pipeline turns video into a photoreal 3D scene with metadata preserved.

Relive in XR

Step back in with Apple Vision Pro or Meta Quest—walk, pause, listen, remember.

Share on MemoPortal

Invite family and friends on web. Embed scenes, post to Instagram, co-create.

  1. 1 Open MemoLens and follow the ring guide.
  2. 2 Record one 360° loop per height (x5) with countdown cues.
  3. 3 Auto-upload for 3DGS reconstruction. Review the preview.
  4. 4 Explore in MemoSpace XR or share via MemoPortal.
